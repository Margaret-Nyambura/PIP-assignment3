Our History


SWEET CAKE BAKERY was founded in 2007 by chef Gabriel and his wife Garnella.

started as a small snack shop but over the years, it has grown into being the biggest

bakery shop in the community. We offer baking services for birthdays, weddings, family and

communal occassions and also family cakes. From the beginning of our bakery, we have created employement

employment to over 200 youths in our community.


Without no doubts, SWEET CAKES BAKERY is your ONE STOP BAKERY STORE!
